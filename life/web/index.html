Project - LIFE

Last-minute Intervention, Full Existence

Project - LIFE is a computer-vision guardian that watches a camera feed, recognizes life-threatening moments the instant they happen — a person falling, fire or smoke, an armed threat — then raises the alarm and dispatches the right responders. When seconds decide outcomes, Project - LIFE buys back the minutes that matter.

Capabilities
What LIFE sees

Each hazard is an independent detector. Signals are confirmed over time, then fused into an incident with a severity and a responder.

🧍

Fall detection

MediaPipe pose tracks torso angle, downward velocity, and body shape. A state machine confirms a genuine collapse and rejects sitting or bending.

🔥

Fire & smoke

A YOLO fire model when available, with a color + flicker heuristic fallback so ignition is caught even without custom weights.

⚠️

Weapon & threat

Detects weapons and escalates to a critical threat when one appears close to a person — not just presence, but intent in context.

🧠

Fusion & severity

A declarative rules engine maps signals to incidents, boosts severity for dangerous combinations (fire + person), and debounces duplicates.

📟

Instant dispatch

Pluggable adapters alert medical, fire, or police via console, webhook, email, or SMS. A local siren sounds until acknowledged.

🔒

Local & private

Runs on a normal laptop CPU. No cloud upload by default — frames and snapshots stay on the device you control.

Pipeline
How it works

A single real-time loop, from pixels to responders.

01

Capture

Webcam, RTSP, or a video file is read frame-by-frame into the engine.

02

Detect

Pose, fire, and weapon detectors analyze each frame in parallel.

03

Fuse

Signals are confirmed over time and combined into a severity-ranked incident.

04

Alert

The siren fires and the right responder is notified with a snapshot.
